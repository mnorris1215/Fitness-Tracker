Success Through Pain

Get Started
To live is to suffer, to survive is to find meaning in the suffering.

-Friedrich Nietzsche

We are created from pain and molded through suffering. A hard and guiding truth. We hope to trade our pains for the few happy reprieves we take in life. The better we are at trading our pains, the more satisfied we become.

"Why?" you may ask? Because, the world is imperfect. When there is imperfection, suffering exists. Yet when there is there exists a knowledge of something superior, and soonthe skills to advance to greatness.
